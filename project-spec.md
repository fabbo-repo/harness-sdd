# project-spec.md — &lt;your-project&gt;

> A **conversed** specification, not a dictated one. Each section is born from
> a debate between the human and the `spec_partner`: what it does, what the
> exact contract is, what edge cases exist, and what alternatives were
> discarded and why. From here the `gherkin_author` distills
> `features/<name>.feature`.
>
> This file starts empty. The `spec_partner` fills it, one feature at a time,
> as you converse. Delete this template text once you have real content.

## Global decisions

_Cross-cutting choices that apply to every feature (dependencies, storage,
error contract, data shapes, …). Record the decision **and its rationale**,
plus the discarded alternative and why it lost._

- _Example shape:_ **&lt;decision&gt;.** &lt;what and why&gt;. *Discarded alternative:*
  &lt;option&gt; — &lt;why it was rejected&gt;.

behavior, the exact contract (inputs → stdout / stderr / exit code), edge
cases, and the decisions with their rationale._
